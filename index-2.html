About Us

LST is not just another NGO but a dream and vision to give Humanity not only a new meaning but a true meaning. Humans have, over the years, grown scientifically & digitally and are now more than connected with each other than ever before but ironically and very very sadly they have lost connections with humanity and....themselves.
LST endeavors to rekindle the hidden goodness amongst all the living souls and connect humans with humans through humanity.
We have lived with issues pertaining to poverty, hunger, unemployment, basic education, health & hygiene, skill development, child labour, sexual harassment, etc but even after billions being spent on them the problem still persists and has only been growing rapidly – all due to fewer helping hands as Governments alone cannot do everything.
LST invites and welcomes helping hands who can collaborate physically &/or financially in addressing the issues and making a better society to live.
